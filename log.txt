
Command: node liri.js -h
Usage: node liri.js [arguments]

Options:
  my-tweets                             show last 20 tweets and when they were created
  spotify-this-song '<song name here>'  show the song name, artist(s), album, and preview URL
  movie-this '<movie name here>'        show the movie title, year, IMDB rating, country,
                                        language, plot and actors
  do-what-it-says                       reads random.txt with arguments separated by a comma
